What to expect from the show?

From the six shows that Gorillaz have already played in Latin America in recent weeks, it is possible to get an idea of what will be presented on the Tour. The skeleton of the setlist is similar, despite the space for small new features, as was the case in Mexico (Saturnz Barz), Chile (The Pink Phantom), Uruguay (Cracker Island, Silent Running, Rockit and more).

Tour Dates and Tickets

The band is made up of four lively members: 2-D, Murdoc, Noodle and Russell. The group's music is the result of collaboration between several other musicians, with Damon Albarn being the only permanent member. Their musical style is typically classified as alternative rock, although there is a lot of influence from Britpop, dub and electronica.
